ABOUT US

To raise awareness about the essential role of trees in building livable and sustainable communities while addressing the urgent issue of deforestation. Trees provide countless benefits. They clean the air, regulate temperatures, conserve water, and support biodiversity. However, deforestation threatens these vital contributions, putting our planetâ€™s health and future at risk.


We envision a world where communities understand the value of trees and actively protect them to create healthier, greener spaces. By preserving forests and planting more trees, we can enhance the quality of life, combat climate change, and ensure a sustainable environment for future generations. Our dream is a planet where trees are at the heart of thriving, harmonious communities.


Our Mission

Our mission is to create resilient urban environments by preserving and expanding tree coverage, fostering community engagement, and promoting sustainable practices in urban forestry to combat climate change and enhance the well-being of cities and their residents.

Our Mission

To preserve and expand tree coverage, foster community engagement, and promote sustainable urban forestry practices that will help mitigate climate change and improve urban resilience.


Our Impact

With the support of volunteers and partners, we have planted over 15,000 trees in urban areas, improved green space accessibility, and organized educational campaigns that have reached thousands of city residents, enhancing environmental awareness and policy advocacy.
