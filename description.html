The Oak tree is a sturdy and long-lived species with lobed leaves and acorns. Its strong branches make it an iconic park feature.



Uses : Providing shade, supporting wildlife, timber production.

Famous for its vibrant autumn foliage in shades of red, orange, and yellow, the Maple tree is an ornamental favorite.



Uses : Aesthetic purposes, syrup production, shade.

Found in tropical and subtropical regions, Palm trees have tall, slender trunks and fan-like leaves.



Uses: Landscaping, providing coconuts or dates, adding a tropical aesthetic.
